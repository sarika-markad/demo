Chocolate cake recipe

This chocolate cake is easy to make and turns our moist and delicous every time. It's perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or when you want a sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the over to 180°C (350°F)
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, and cocoa powder together
  3. Add eggs, and milk, and then stir until smooth
  4. Pour the mixture into baking tin
  5. Bake for 30 minutes. Let cool before serving
